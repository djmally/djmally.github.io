About Me

I'm currently a software engineer at Synack, where I work on distributed systems technology and web injection frameworks as part of our Hydra product. In 2016, I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with my bachelor's & master's degrees in computer science.

At Penn, I was a part of the Science & Technology Wing (STWing), as well as WQHS, Penn's student-run radio station. Academically, I was a TA for CIS {110, 240, 371}, and I co-taught CIS 198: Rust Programming in the spring 2016 semester with Kai Ninomiya & Terry Sun.
