About Me

Hi! I'm David. I'm a security engineer at Airtable, and I used to do web fuzzing and distributed systems engineering at Synack. I studied computer science at Penn, with a focus on programming languages and computer security.

At Penn, I was a part of the Science & Technology Wing (STWing), as well as WQHS, Penn's student-run radio station. Academically, I was a teaching assistant for Introduction to Computer Programming (CIS 110), Introduction to Computer Architecture (CIS 240), and Computer Organization and Design (CIS 371), and I co-taught CIS 198: Rust Programming in the spring 2016 semester with Kai Ninomiya & Terry Sun.
